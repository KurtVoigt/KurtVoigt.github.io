Tic-Tac-Toe

This is a Tic-Tac-Toe which implements the Minimax algorithm with a slight twist.

Namely, since tic tac toe is a game with a small move set, I decided not to put a look-ahead mechanism in. This means that that the algorithm will simulate all possible iterations of the game and pick the optimal move. It has a strong bias towards winning, a small bias towards a draw, and a strong bias against losing. basically, it is impossible to win :)
